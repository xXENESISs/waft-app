WAFT Combat Guide

Core combat systems, stats and biome rules

Combat Attributes

  • Life: Total health.
  • Attack: Base damage.
  • Defense: Damage reduction.
  • Resistance: Determines stamina.
  • Speed: Initiative and contributes to accuracy/evasion.
  • Agility: Main evasion stat.
  • Technique: Main precision stat.
  • Explosiveness: Critical potential.

Life System

Total HP = Life × 10

Initiative

Speed determines who acts first.

Damage Formula

D = Attack − Defense

Multiplier (%) = 75 + (D ÷ 4)

Damage = Attack × (Multiplier ÷ 100)

Accuracy and Evasion

Precision = Technique × 0.8 + Speed × 0.2

Evasion = Agility × 0.8 + Speed × 0.2

Base Hit Chance (%) = 60 + (Precision − Evasion) × 0.8

Stamina and Fatigue

Max Stamina = Resistance × 10

  • > 50% → no penalty
  • ≤ 50% → −10%
  • ≤ 25% → −20%
  • ≤ 10% → −50%

Explosiveness and Criticals

Crit Chance = Explosiveness × 0.4

Crit Damage = ×1.5

Combat Actions

Normal Attack

Technique × 0.8 + Speed × 0.2

Quick Attack

Agility × 0.6 + Technique × 0.2 + Speed × 0.2

Precise Attack

Technique × 0.9 + Speed × 0.1

+10% damage

Explosive Attack

Explosiveness × 0.6 + Technique × 0.2 + Speed × 0.2

Concentration

  • +10% Defense
  • +20 Life
  • +20 Stamina

Restrictions:

  • Cannot be used two turns in a row
  • Cannot be used under status effects
  • Exceptions: Bleed, Bite, Perforation
  • Can be used again if a climate effect changes

Status Effects

Bleed: Damage over time. Used by: Tiger

Poison: Damage over time. Used by: Kaiser’s Spotted Newt

Tetrodotoxin (TTX): −25% Precision, Evasion and Speed. Used by: Pufferfish, Japanese Fire-Bellied Newt

Perforation: Heavy damage over time. Used by: White Rhino, Narwhal

Bite: −20% Evasion. Used by: Black Caiman

Anchor: Life and Mana drain. Used by: Tick

Mimicry: Reduces incoming damage until hit. Used by: Mossy Frog

Environmental Effects

Hail: Damage per turn. Used by: Walrus

Sandstorm: Evasion modifiers and survivability shifts. Used by: Fennec Fox

Humidity: Increased Mana pressure and Life regeneration. Used by: Green Iguana

Day/Night Cycle: Alternating combat states that change damage, evasion and ability access. Used by: Eurasian Eagle-Owl
