Green Iguana

Iguana iguana

Region: Northern South America

Habitat: Tropical forests, riverbanks, mangroves and humid lowland environments

Martial Representation: Colombian Grima

The Green Iguana embodies flexibility, balance and evasive body intelligence. Agile, long-limbed and highly coordinated, it thrives through angular movement, posture disruption and fluid mid-range exchanges. In combat, it relies on timing, deceptive repositioning and technical control rather than brute force.

Martial Discipline Overview

Colombian Grima is a traditional Afro-Colombian stick and blade-based fighting system built around rhythm, footwork, deceptive cadence and angular attack patterns. It emphasizes mobility, tactical timing and continuous adaptation through flowing offensive and defensive transitions. Within WAFT, it enhances agility, technique and positional unpredictability.

Combat Abilities

Passive Ability

Oppressive Humidity

After using 3 Special Attacks, humidity is activated for 4 turns (8 turns in Jungle biome).

During humidity, the opponent’s mana costs increase by +5 and Iguana restores 10 Life per turn.

Special Attack

Refresh

Restores 30 Life and Mana, or 60 if humidity is active.

Also applies −20% Precision and −20% Evasion to the opponent for 1 turn.

Ultimate Charge: Offensive (3 hits)
