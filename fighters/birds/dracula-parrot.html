Dracula Parrot

Psittrichas fulgidus

Region: New Guinea

Habitat: Mountain forests, humid highlands and dense tropical woodland

Martial Representation: Papuan Highlands Spear Fighting

The Dracula Parrot embodies ritual menace, battlefield presence and disciplined tribal aggression. Dark, imposing and visually unforgettable, it dominates through intimidation, strong defensive framing and committed weapon pressure. In combat, it thrives in controlled mid-range exchanges where spear reach, shield structure and relentless forward intent shape the fight.

Martial Discipline Overview

Papuan Highlands Spear Fighting represents a tribal combat tradition centered on spear use, shield defense, timing and direct territorial pressure. It favors committed strikes, guarded advance and practical battlefield control over ornamental movement. Within WAFT, it enhances reach, defensive structure and forceful mid-range engagement.

Combat Abilities

Passive Ability

Crimson Feast

On hit, Dracula Parrot applies Bleed, up to a maximum of 3 stacks. Each stack deals 10 damage and heals Dracula Parrot for 10 Life at the end of the turn.

Bleed is removed by Concentration, which also grants immunity to new Bleed stacks during that turn.

Special Attack

Scarlet Ritual

Deals 30 damage and restores 30 Life per active Bleed stack, up to a maximum of 90, then consumes all stacks.

This attack cannot miss.

Ultimate Charge: Offensive (4 hits)
