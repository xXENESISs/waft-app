Peregrine Falcon

Falco peregrinus

Region: Korea and wider Eurasia

Habitat: Cliffs, open highlands, mountains and coastal escarpments

Martial Representation: Taekkyeon

The Peregrine Falcon embodies flowing mobility, abrupt acceleration and elegant striking rhythm. Light, reactive and difficult to pin down, it thrives through timing, angle changes and fast offensive entry rather than prolonged brute exchanges. In combat, it favors fluid control and sudden bursts of pressure.

Martial Discipline Overview

Taekkyeon is a traditional Korean martial art built around rhythmic footwork, flowing movement and dynamic kicking. It emphasizes balance disruption, deceptive timing and fluid transitions rather than rigid force. Within WAFT, it enhances speed, agility and mobile close-to-mid range striking.

Combat Abilities

Passive Ability

Hunting Momentum

Each successful hit grants +10% damage to the next one, up to a maximum of 3 stacks.

Missing an attack resets all stored stacks.

Special Attack

Fatal Dive

Reduces the opponent’s hit chance to 25% for that turn, then attacks for 1.5× base damage.

If it lands, the damage scales up to 1.8× depending on stored stacks, applies −25% Precision and −25% Evasion for 2 turns, and consumes all stacks.

Ultimate Charge: Offensive (3 hits)
