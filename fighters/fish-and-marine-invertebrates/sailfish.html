Sailfish

Istiophorus platypterus

Region: Tropical and subtropical oceans

Habitat: Open ocean, warm offshore waters and pelagic zones

Martial Representation: Lethwei

The Sailfish embodies violent acceleration, relentless offense and razor-sharp forward commitment. Built for blistering bursts and predatory interception, it does not wait for the fight to settle: it crashes into it with speed, pressure and ruthless intent. In combat, it thrives through explosive entry, aggressive momentum and overwhelming offensive tempo.

Martial Discipline Overview

Lethwei is a traditional Burmese striking art known for extreme aggression, full-contact exchanges and raw offensive pressure. It rewards resilience, explosive commitment and the willingness to break rhythm through sheer violence. Within WAFT, it enhances attack, explosiveness and direct close-range assault.

Combat Abilities

Passive Ability

Marine Echo

When attacking, there is a 50% chance to strike again in Marine biome, or 20% in other biomes.

The second hit deals full damage and uses Speed as its Precision stat, and cannot critically strike.

Special Attack

Phantom Current

Becomes untargetable for that turn. After the opponent’s action, it strikes with double damage using Speed as Precision.

Cooldown: 3 turns

Ultimate Charge: Offensive (3 hits)
