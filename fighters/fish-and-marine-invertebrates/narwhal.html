Narwhal

Monodon monoceros

Region: Arctic waters of Canada, Greenland and Russia

Habitat: Cold marine environments, polar waters, drifting ice zones and deep Arctic coastal seas

Martial Representation: Okichitaw with Tomahawk

The Narwhal is a powerful mid-range pressure fighter built around precision entry, disciplined weapon mechanics and heavy forward commitment. Durable and technically refined, it combines controlled offensive structure with the threat of sudden penetrating force. In combat, it excels at measured engagement, timing-based disruption and decisive committed strikes rather than evasive fluidity.

Martial Discipline Overview

Okichitaw is a modern Indigenous martial system inspired by Plains Cree combat traditions, structured around movement discipline, weapon integration and efficient tactical engagement. With the tomahawk, it emphasizes angular strikes, committed entries, defensive readiness and practical combat flow. Within WAFT, it reinforces attack power, technical precision and controlled offensive pressure.
