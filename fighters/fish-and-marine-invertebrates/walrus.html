Walrus

Odobenus rosmarus

Region: Arctic Circle

Habitat: Polar coasts, sea ice, rocky shores and freezing marine environments

Martial Representation: Chukotkan Harpoon Combat

The Walrus embodies brutal mass, cold-environment resilience and relentless forward pressure. Heavy, scarred and terrifying at close range, it dominates through crushing body commitment, raw momentum and harsh battlefield endurance. In combat, it thrives by forcing contact, absorbing punishment and turning every exchange into a contest of force and survival.

Martial Discipline Overview

Chukotkan Harpoon Combat is represented in WAFT as a harsh northern fighting tradition centered on hunting weapons, close-quarters brutality and practical survival violence. It emphasizes timing, committed thrusting power, environmental toughness and direct confrontation under unforgiving conditions. Within WAFT, it enhances attack force, durability and ruthless short-range combat pressure.

Combat Abilities

Passive Ability

Frozen Impact

Critical hits reduce the opponent’s Evasion by 20% for 2 turns, or 4 turns in Arctic biome.

Special Attack

Arctic Storm

Activates hail for 3 turns (6 turns in Arctic biome).

During hail, all fighters lose 15 Life per turn, while Walrus restores 15 Life per turn.

Additionally, Walrus gains double Speed. In Arctic biome, this Speed bonus becomes permanent.

Ultimate Charge: Defensive (4 hits)
