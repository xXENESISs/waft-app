Sumatran Tiger

Panthera tigris sumatrae

Region: Sumatra, Indonesia

Habitat: Tropical forests, dense jungle and mountainous rainforest

Martial Representation: Pencak Silat Harimau

The Sumatran Tiger embodies low-line ferocity, coiled explosiveness and predatory precision. Built for sudden pressure and lethal close-range engagement, it thrives through balance disruption, angular entry and violent finishing potential. In combat, it is both elegant and terrifyingly direct.

Martial Discipline Overview

Pencak Silat Harimau is a Sumatran branch of silat known for its low stances, grounded mobility, sweeping attacks and predatory entry angles. It emphasizes explosive transitions, destabilization and close-range domination. Within WAFT, it enhances agility, technical aggression and short-range pressure.
