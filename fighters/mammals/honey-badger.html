Honey Badger

Mellivora capensis

Region: Sub-Saharan Africa

Habitat: Savannas, dry forests and semi-arid regions

Martial Representation: Krav Maga

The Honey Badger represents relentless forward aggression. Durable, uncompromising and built for high-impact exchanges, it thrives in medium-to-close range confrontations where pressure and resilience overwhelm refined distance control.

Martial Discipline Overview

Krav Maga is a modern self-defense system built around efficiency, directness and controlled aggression. It emphasizes rapid neutralization of threats, close-range engagement and decisive counterattacks. Within WAFT, it enhances explosive entry and pressure-based exchanges.
