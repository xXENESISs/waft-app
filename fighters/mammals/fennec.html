Fennec Fox

Vulpes zerda

Region: North Africa

Habitat: Sahara Desert and arid regions

Martial Representation: Tahtib

The Fennec Fox represents technical mobility and distance discipline. Light, precise and difficult to pin down, it thrives in long-range exchanges where timing, spatial control and clean execution matter more than raw force.

Martial Discipline Overview

Tahtib is a traditional Egyptian stick-fighting art centered on distance control, rhythm and disciplined striking. It emphasizes timing, precision and tactical positioning rather than brute force. Within WAFT, it enhances reach management, technical accuracy and long-range engagement.

Combat Abilities

Passive Ability

Desert Mastery

Each hit generates charges, with 2 charges per hit in Desert biome. Upon reaching 8 charges, a sandstorm is activated for 6 turns.

During the sandstorm, the opponent suffers −25% Evasion, the Fennec gains +25% Evasion, and restores 10 Life per turn.

Special Attack

Mirage Staff

A Precise attack. During sandstorm, it cannot miss, deals +100% damage, and restores 50 Life.

Ultimate Charge: Offensive (4 hits)
