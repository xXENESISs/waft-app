Dung Beetle

Scarabaeus sacer

Region: North Africa, Southern Europe and Western Asia

Habitat: Dry open terrain, scrublands, woodland edges and warm terrestrial environments

Martial Representation: Pankration

The Dung Beetle is a compact powerhouse built for collision, leverage and relentless physical control. Dense, durable and brutally force-oriented, it excels in close-range domination where pressure, grappling strength and structural disruption decide the exchange. In combat, it pushes forward with crushing intent, turning every clash into a test of impact tolerance and raw control.

Martial Discipline Overview

Pankration was an ancient Greek full-contact combat system combining striking, grappling, takedowns and submission-oriented control with minimal restriction. Built for total fighting efficiency, it rewarded toughness, positional dominance and the ability to transition seamlessly between ranges. Within WAFT, it reinforces attack power, physical control and all-around close-quarters brutality.

Combat Abilities

Passive Ability

Momentum

Consecutive successful hits increase damage in sequence: +5%, +10%, +15%, and +20% maximum.

Missing or skipping an attack resets the chain completely.

Special Attack

Rolling Throw

Uses Attack as its Precision stat, deals normal damage, and applies −50% Evasion for 3 turns.

This attack counts as a successful hit for the purpose of maintaining Momentum.

Ultimate Charge: Offensive (3 hits)
