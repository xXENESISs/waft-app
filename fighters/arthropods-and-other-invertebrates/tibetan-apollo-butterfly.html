Tibetan Apollo Butterfly

Parnassius epaphus

Region: Himalayas

Habitat: Alpine meadows, rocky mountain slopes and cold high-altitude terrain

Martial Representation: Wushu with Sheng Biao

The Tibetan Apollo Butterfly represents extreme agility, deceptive motion and high-altitude precision. Light, elusive and difficult to read, it fights through rhythm disruption, rapid directional changes and sudden weapon-based strikes from unexpected angles. In combat, it thrives on spacing, elegance and technical unpredictability rather than raw physical power.

Martial Discipline Overview

Wushu with sheng biao emphasizes flowing body mechanics, elastic movement, deceptive timing and long-range control through a flexible dart-rope weapon. It rewards precision, coordination and sudden directional changes over brute force. Within WAFT, it enhances agility, technique, distance manipulation and fluid offensive transitions.

Combat Abilities

Passive Ability

High-Altitude Breathing

When using Concentration, Tibetan Apollo Butterfly gains +20% Defense, restores 20 Life and 20 Mana, and gains +20% Evasion.

In Mountain or Arctic biome, all these effects are multiplied by 1.5.

The effect lasts 1 turn and cannot stack.

Special Attack

Tibetan Nectar

Choose one of three effects: deal 50 damage, restore 50 Life, or restore 50 Mana.

In a favorable biome, the chosen value becomes 75 instead.

Ultimate Charge: Defensive (2 hits)
