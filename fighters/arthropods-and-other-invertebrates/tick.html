Tick

Ixodida

Region: Global

Habitat: Forest floors, dense vegetation, humid undergrowth and parasitic host environments

Martial Representation: Traditional Japanese Jujutsu

The Tick embodies persistence, attachment control and suffocating close-range technique. Small, durable and unnervingly committed, it thrives through pressure, body manipulation and relentless structural disruption. In combat, it excels at staying attached, forcing uncomfortable grappling exchanges and turning short-range control into slow domination.

Martial Discipline Overview

Traditional Japanese Jujutsu emphasizes joint manipulation, throws, locks, off-balancing and close-range tactical control. It is designed around efficiency, structural breaks and the exploitation of vulnerable positions rather than prolonged striking. Within WAFT, it enhances technique, defensive control and punishing grappling transitions.

Combat Abilities

Passive Ability

Parasitic Anchor

On hit, Tick has a 50% chance to Anchor itself to the opponent.

Anchor: deals 20 Life damage and drains 5 Mana per turn, both of which are stolen by Tick.

The opponent has a 50% chance to break free at the end of each turn.

Special Attack

Total Absorption

Can only be used while the opponent is Anchored.

Tick detaches, steals 70 Life and 20 Mana, then attacks and may Anchor again.

Ultimate Charge: Offensive (3 hits)
