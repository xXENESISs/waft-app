Mossy Frog

Theloderma corticale

Region: Vietnam

Habitat: Humid forest streams, mossy rocks and shaded jungle wetlands

Martial Representation: Võ Tây Sơn with Long Staff

The Mossy Frog embodies patient concealment, rooted balance and sudden controlled extension. Built to disappear into wet forest terrain and strike from stillness, it thrives through measured spacing, deceptive calm and disciplined weapon use. In combat, it favors structured mid-range control and sharp, well-timed interruption rather than reckless forward pressure.

Martial Discipline Overview

Võ Tây Sơn is a Vietnamese martial tradition associated with practical striking, weapon discipline and strong structural movement. The long staff extends its tactical reach, rewarding timing, line control and committed interception. Within WAFT, it enhances reach, defensive spacing and composed mid-range pressure.

Combat Abilities

Passive Ability

Living Texture

When using Concentration, Mossy Frog enters mimicry. While active, incoming attacks deal only 70% of their normal damage.

This effect does not activate in Desert or Arctic biome, and is lost immediately when Mossy Frog is hit.

Special Attack

Moss Absorption

Restores 25 Life and Mana, and blocks critical hits and the opponent’s Special Attack for that turn.

For the next 2 turns, restores 25 Life and 25 Mana at the end of each turn.

Ultimate Charge: Defensive (3 hits)
